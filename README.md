# Diwali Sales Analysis

An  data analytics project analyzing Diwali sales patterns to uncover customer behavior and business insights.

## Overview
This project explores a Diwali sales dataset to identify purchasing trends across gender, age groups, marital status, occupation, and product categories. It includes data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualizations, insights, and business recommendations.
## Problem Statement
Businesses experience high sales during festive seasons like Diwali but lack clarity on which customers buy the most, what products perform well, and which demographic segment contributes the highest revenue.
This project aims to answer:

-Who buys the most during the Diwali season?

-Which demographic group contributes the highest revenue?

-What occupations and age groups should be targeted for marketing?

-What products sell the most?
## Dataset
-File Name: Diwali Sales Data.csv

-Columns: Gender, Age Group, Marital Status, Occupation, Product ID, Amount, etc.

-Contains: Customer demographics, purchase behavior, and spending details

## Results & Conclusion
- Women, especially aged 26–35, are the primary buyers during Diwali.

- Married females have the highest spending power.

- Customers from IT, Healthcare, and Aviation dominate purchases.

- Businesses should focus marketing campaigns on these segments for maximum sales impact.

- Best-selling products indicate strong interest in popular product IDs such as P00265242 and P00110942.
